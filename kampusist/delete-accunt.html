Kampusist Account Deletion Guide

Users can permanently delete their Kampusist account directly from within the application. Once the deletion request is submitted, the account deletion process will begin and the user's personal information will be removed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

How to Delete Your Account

Step 1: Open Your Profile

Launch the Kampusist application and navigate to the Profile tab located in the bottom navigation menu.

Step 2: Open the Menu Section

Inside the Profile page, scroll down to the menu section where account-related settings are available.

Step 3: Select "Delete Account"

Tap the Delete Account option. This will open the account deletion request screen.

Step 4: Choose a Reason

Select the reason for deleting your account. This feedback helps us improve Kampusist for future users.

  • I no longer use Kampusist.
  • I created another account.
  • Privacy concerns.
  • Technical issues.
  • Other.

Step 5: Confirm Deletion Request

After selecting a reason, confirm your request by tapping Submit Deletion Request.

Your request will be recorded and your account deletion process will begin.

What Happens After Submission?

Important:
  • Your account will no longer be accessible.
  • Your profile information will be deleted or anonymized.
  • Your posts, comments, and content may be removed according to our policies.
  • Certain information may be retained where legally required.
