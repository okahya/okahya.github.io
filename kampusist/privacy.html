Privacy Policy for Kampusist

Last Updated: June 2026

Kampusist ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Kampusist mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account Information such as username, email address, profile information, and other information voluntarily provided by users.
  • Device Information including device identifier (Device ID), operating system version, device model, and application version.
  • Location Information when permission is granted by the user.
  • Analytics Data regarding app usage, performance, crashes, and user interactions.
  • Advertising Identifiers used to display and measure advertisements.
  • User-Generated Content such as posts, comments, messages, profile details, and other content submitted through the App.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information may be used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Provide location-based features.
  • Personalize user experience and content.
  • Display advertisements.
  • Analyze user behavior and improve performance.
  • Detect fraud, abuse, security incidents, and technical issues.
  • Send notifications and service-related communications.

3. Notifications

Kampusist may send push notifications to inform users about important updates, messages, events, announcements, reminders, and other application-related activities. Users may disable notifications at any time through their device settings.

4. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar technologies to understand how users interact with the App and improve our services. These services may collect technical and usage-related information.

5. Advertising

Kampusist may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob. These providers may use advertising identifiers and similar technologies to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements and measure advertising performance.

6. Location Information

With your permission, Kampusist may access and process location information to provide location-based features and improve user experience. Location access may be revoked at any time through device settings.

7. Data Storage and Retention

To provide our services, user information may be stored on secure servers and cloud infrastructure. We retain data for as long as necessary to operate the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and provide requested services.

Certain content and account-related information may remain stored until the user deletes their account or requests deletion of specific data.

8. User Data Deletion Rights

Users have the right to delete their account and personal data. When an account deletion request is submitted through the App or by contacting us, we will take reasonable steps to remove or anonymize personal information, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Users may also request deletion of specific content or personal information associated with their account.

The App may integrate with third-party service providers including but not limited to:

These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no electronic storage system or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Kampusist is intended solely for individuals who are at least 18 years of age. Persons under the age of 18 are not permitted to use the App.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years old. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Depending on your jurisdiction and applicable laws, including GDPR, KVKK, and other privacy regulations, you may have the right to:
